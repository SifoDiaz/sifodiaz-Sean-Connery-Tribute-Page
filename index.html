Sir Thomas Sean Connery

Best known as the first actor to portray fictional British secret agent 007, James Bond, in the hugely successful movie franchise. Starring in seven films between 1962 and 1983.

Sean Connery as James Bond 007 posing with the Aston Martin DB5 from the film GOLDFINGER, 1964.

Remembering Sean Connery's Life

August 25, 1930 - October 31, 2020

Timeline of Sean Connery's Achievements

  • 1930 - Born in Fountainbridge, Edinburgh, Scotland. His mother, Euphamia C. Maclean, was a cleaning lady, and his father, Joseph Connery, was a factory worker and lorry driver.
  • 1946 - At the age of 16, Connery joined the Royal Navy. He received two tattoos on his arm, reading: "MUM AND DAD" and "SCOTLAND FOREVER."
  • 1950 - His hobby of bodybuilding culminated in a bid for the 1950 Mr. Universe title where he placed third.
  • 1953 - At the age of 23, he had a choice between becoming a professional footballer or an actor, and even though he showed much promise in the sport, he chose acting and said it was one of his more intelligent moves.
  • 1962 - Connery's breakthrough came in the role of British secret agent James Bond. Perfomed in movie Dr No.
  • 1963 - Performed in movie From Russia with Love as James Bond.
  • 1964 - Performed in movie Goldfinger as James Bond.
  • 1965 - Performed in movie Thunderball as James Bond. Also received Golden Laurel.
  • 1967 - Performed in movie You Only Live Twice as James Bond.
  • 1971 - Performed in movie Diamonds Are Forever as James Bond.
  • 1975 - Married to Moroccan-French painter Micheline Roquebrune (born 1929) until his death in October 2020.
  • 1977 - Performed in movie A Bridge Too Far as Maj. Gen. Robert E. Urquhart.
  • 1983 - Performed in movie Never Say Never Again as James Bond.
  • 1987 - Performed in movie The Untouchables as Jim Malone, received Golden Globe Award for Best Supporting Actor and Academy Award for Best Actor in a Supporting Role. Received London Film Critics Circle Award for Actor of the Year, and German Film Award for Best Actor.
  • 1988 - Received BAFTA Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role.
  • 1989 - Performed in movie Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade as Professor Henry Jones.
  • 1990 - Performed in movie The Hunt for Red October as Marko Ramius.
  • 1993 - Performed in movie Rising Sun as Capt. John Connor.
  • 1996 - Performed in movie The Rock as John Patrick Mason.
  • 1998 - Performed in movie The Avengers as Sir August de Wynter.
  • 2000 - Veteran James Bond actor Sean Connery, knighted by Britain's Queen Elizabeth II. Two years after he reportedly was denied a knighthood because of his passionate Scottish nationalism.
  • 2003 - Performed in movie The League 0f Extraordinary Gentlemen as Allan Quatermain.
  • 2006 - Received the American Film Institute's Lifetime Achievement Award and announces decision to retire from acting.
  • 2020 - Connery died in his sleep on 31 October 2020, aged 90, at his home in the Lyford Cay community of Nassau in The Bahamas.
